These posters can be used to help raise awareness about healthy contact lens wear and care. They are designed for contact lens wearers. Display these posters in highly visible areas—eye and healthcare provider offices and waiting rooms, college campus dorms and health centers, and schools—where contact lens wearers will see them and be reminded to practice healthy habits.

General Contact Lens Wear and Care

Eye Wise - Contact Lenses 101: Don’t Be a Stranger—Visit Your Eye Doctor Every Year

Message: Schedule a visit with your eye doctor at least once a year. Take out your contacts and call your eye doctor if you have eye pain, discomfort, redness, or blurry vision.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Don’t be a stranger—Visit Your Eye Doctor Every Year [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: Don’t be a stranger—Visit Your Eye Doctor Every Year [PDF - 2 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise - Contact Lenses 101: Handy Tip—Wash Your Hands Before Touching Your Contacts

Message: Wash your hands with soap and water and dry them with a clean towel before touching your contacts.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Handy Tip—Wash Your Hands Before Touching Your Contacts [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: Handy Tip—Wash Your Hands Before Touching Your Contacts [PDF - 2 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise - Contact Lenses 101: Stay on Schedule: Replace Your Contact Lenses Regularly

Message: Remember to keep supplies on hand and replace your contacts as often as your eye doctor tells you.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Stay on Schedule: Replace Your Contact Lenses Regularly [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: Stay on Schedule: Replace Your Contact Lenses Regularly [PDF - 2 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise - Contact Lenses 101: Clean and Replace That Grimy Case

Message: Rinse your case with solution, rub to clean it, and store it upside down every day. Get a new case at least every three months.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Clean and Replace That Grimy Case [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: Clean and Replace That Grimy Case [PDF - 2 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise – Contact Lenses 101: Sleeping in Contacts is Risky Business

Message: Take out your contact lenses before sleeping, unless your doctor specifically tells you otherwise.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Sleeping in Contacts is Risky Business [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: Sleeping in Contacts is Risky Business [PDF - 2 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise – Contact Lenses 101: No H2O – Keep Water Away From Contacts

Message: Don’t rinse or store contacts in water, and avoid showering or swimming while wearing contacts.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: No H2O – Keep Water Away From Contacts [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)

Contact Lenses 101: No H2O – Keep Water Away From Contacts [PDF - 1 MB] (11 x 17)

Eye Wise – Contact Lenses 101: Keep it Fresh – Only Use New Solution

Message: Dump all the solution out of your contact case every time you use it, and only store your contacts in fresh solution.
Audience: Designed for young adult contact lens wearers.

Posters:

Contact Lenses 101: Keep it Fresh – Only Use New Solution [PDF - 1 MB] (8.5 x 11)
