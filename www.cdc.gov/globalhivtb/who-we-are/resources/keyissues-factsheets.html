Fact Sheets on Key Issues

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

While the world has made great progress in the fight against HIV and TB, there is an urgent need to do more. Working side by side with Ministries of Health and other partners, CDC uses a data-driven approach to deliver effective, long-term HIV prevention, treatment and care and find innovative ways to find, cure and prevent TB worldwide. Included below are several fact sheets that detail CDC’s priorities to fight both HIV and TB globally, and ultimately, bring an end to both epidemics.
