Spirometry Training Program

Search the NIOSH-Approved Spirometry Schedule

Sponsors teach courses regionally, nationally, and internationally. A search option is provided so that you may find a course scheduled nearest you. The course schedules are very dynamic, and may not be entirely up to date. Be sure to verify the location and timing of courses with the sponsor. Some course sponsors offer the option for students to register for a class on the sponsor's website. Some sponsors also will travel to a requestor's site to give a course. Contact the individual sponsor to make these arrangements.
