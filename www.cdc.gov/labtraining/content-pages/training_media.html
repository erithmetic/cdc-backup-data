CDC Laboratory Training

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

CDC Laboratory Training consistently produces internal and external laboratory-related products for state, local, national, and international audiences. We strive to provide educational experiences that are more than just passive, point-and-click exercises. Rather, we use highly-trained multimedia developers, videographers, and animators to add interactivity, attractiveness, and real-live applications to our products. Below, you will find a sampling of the works we produce, from video segments and motion graphics, to interface designs and job aids. Feel free to peruse what we do. If you are looking for job aids for a specific course, please select the course name below to be taken to that specific page’s downloadable materials.
